About Full Oppo

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by a team of passionate aerospace engineers, Full Oppo began as a small research and development firm focused on advanced propulsion systems. Over the years, we have grown into a leading provider of innovative aerospace solutions, serving both government and commercial clients. Our commitment to excellence and relentless pursuit of cutting-edge technology have made us a trusted partner in the aerospace industry.

From pioneering work in hypersonic flight to developing sustainable aviation technologies, Full Oppo has consistently pushed the boundaries of what's possible. We are proud of our history and excited about the future of aerospace.

Our Mission

To advance the frontiers of aerospace technology, enabling safer, more efficient, and sustainable air and space travel for all. We strive to be a catalyst for innovation, driving progress and creating value for our customers and the world.

Our Values

  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and constantly seek new and better ways to solve complex challenges.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with the highest ethical standards, building trust and fostering long-term relationships.
  • Excellence: We are committed to delivering exceptional quality in everything we do, exceeding expectations and setting new benchmarks.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of teamwork and work closely with our partners to achieve shared goals.
  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to developing environmentally responsible technologies that minimize our impact on the planet.

Our Achievements

  • 2022: Awarded the "Aerospace Innovation of the Year" for our work on sustainable aviation fuels.
  • 2020: Successfully launched a prototype hypersonic vehicle, achieving Mach 5 flight.
  • 2018: Partnered with NASA on a research project to develop advanced materials for spacecraft.
  • 2015: Received the "National Medal of Technology and Innovation" for our contributions to aerospace engineering.
  • 2010: Developed a revolutionary propulsion system that significantly reduced fuel consumption in commercial aircraft.
